3.1 The "Pit Window" Logic

The Strategy Engine is the custom logic that sits on top of the ML model. It doesn't just predict a number; it makes a decision.

How it works:

    The engine asks the ML model to predict lap times for the next 20 laps.

    It calculates the delta (Δ) between each consecutive lap.

    If the predicted lap time increases by more than a specific Threshold (e.g., 0.8 seconds per lap) compared to a "fresh tire" baseline, the engine triggers a PIT STOP recommendation.
